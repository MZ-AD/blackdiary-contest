How to Participate

  • Follow @BlackDiary1887 on at least one of the following: Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok.
  • You can be a daily winner once per channel.
  • You can be the grand prize winner on more than one channel.
  • Create a post identifying a notable Black person (dead or alive) or place significant to Black culture in your city to feature on @BlackDiary1887.
  • The post can be a static image or a reel up to 15 seconds long.
  • Posts are evaluated on content quality and engagement.
  • Tag @BlackDiary1887 in your post.
  • Include hashtags: #Blackdiary1887HSContest2026 #whoshouldbeonblackdiary1887
