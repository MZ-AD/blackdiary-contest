Part 2 – Essay Contest

Essay Instructions

Identify a notable Black person (dead or alive) or place with significance to Black culture in your city. Write a 250–350 word essay explaining the significance and why you are inspired.

Essays are read and scored on a rolling basis considering the following:

  • Instructions followed
  • Originality
  • Grammar and spelling
  • The number of your social media accounts following @BlackDiary1887 (Maximum is one account each on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok)
